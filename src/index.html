Teapot Not Found Poker

This is a variation of "Jacks Or Better" video poker. Your initial balance is 404 and your goal is to reach exactly 418. In that case you win the game and officially become a teapot)) If you go beyond 418 you balance is set to 404 plus what is beyond 418 to give you another chance to become a teapot. If your balance is below 404 so it's not found and becomes 404 gain.

Poker rules

Paytable

  • Jacks or Better - 1
  • Two Pair - 2
  • Three of a Kind - 3
  • Straight - 4
  • Flush - 6
  • Full House - 9
  • Four of a Kind - 25
  • Straight Flush - 50
  • Royal Flush - 800
